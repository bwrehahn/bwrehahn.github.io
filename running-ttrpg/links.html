Additional Resources

This section has some links that you might find helpful. Please be aware they will open in a new browser window or tab.

Player Survey - This is a survey you can use. Just hand these to the players and ask for their honest opionion. State that this will help improve their experience and possibly avoid any future issues that may arise.

DrivethruRPG.com - This site offers a lot of RPG books for sale in PDF form you can download. It is also good for seeing what types of games are out there.

Amazon.com - This is a great place to buy books, dice, and other gaming materials.

GameGeeksRPG - It is a series on YouTube that reviews RPG games and is a good source for examples of the different types of RPG Games. Some of the episodes have been sponsered by DrivethruRPG.com

 

The Gamers: Dorkness Rising - This is an independent comedy about a Game Master that is having trouble with his group and how he handels them. If you have played RPG's before, you should give this a look. This is the first episode, you can go to the link to view the movie in segments, or buy the DVD on Amazon.com
