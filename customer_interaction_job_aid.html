Quick Reference — De-escalation Phrases
Open with empathy
  • ▸"I completely understand why you're frustrated."
  • ▸"Thank you for bringing this to my attention."
  • ▸"I can see this has caused a real inconvenience."
Offer ownership
  • ▸"I'm going to take personal responsibility for this."
  • ▸"Let me find out exactly what happened."
  • ▸"Here's what I can do for you right now."
When you need time
  • ▸"I want to make sure I handle this correctly for you."
  • ▸"Can I place you on a brief hold while I check?"
  • ▸"I'll follow up within [timeframe] — is that okay?"
Phrases to avoid
  • ✕"That's our policy." (without context)
  • ✕"There's nothing I can do."
  • ✕"You should have…" or "You need to…"
