eLearning Module

Interactive Lectora Module created for the State Park to promote safe hiking.

 

 

 

My Role

Collaborated as part of a team to develop a 10-minute interactive eLearning module for a state park, promoting safe hiking practices to a general public audience. The project followed a full instructional design process, including needs assessment, storyboarding, and scriptwriting, through to filming, editing, and final development in Lectora..

  • Visual Desig with Adobe Photoshop

  • Instructional Design

  • Scriptwriting
  • eLearning Development
